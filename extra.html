    From the formations of Grotto to the release of their debut album.

    • Summer 2013

      Basement Recording Sessions

      Grotto began in Dan Taro's basement the summer before he started highschool. Alec Fliescher played bass and guitar and played shows often with Grotto's resident drummer, Nate Ball. The two joined forces with Taro on an electronic spin on their own projects. They trio got together in Taro's basement many times over the summer recording various ideas and working on remix competitons.

    • 2014-2018

      Composition of Spring Drips

      Grotto's debut album came about because Taro manipulated the recordings from the previous summer and wrote full arrangements around the recording sessions of Ball and Fliescher. Taro worked tirelessly every morning to create and compose on his hour long commute to school on the bus. During the year, Fliescher left Denver and the band became a duo: Taro on electronics and controllers and Ball on percussion.
